Account Deletion Request

Last updated: March 21, 2026

We're sorry to see you go! If you would like to delete your DanceBuddy account and all associated personal data, you can do so by following the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest and most direct way to delete your account is through the DanceBuddy app itself:

  1. Open the DanceBuddy app and log in to your account.
  2. Tap on Profile.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Tap on Manage My Account.
  5. Select Delete Account and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm.

Option 2: Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team via email.

To submit an email request:

Send an email to: support@dancebuddyapp.com

Please include the following information so we can verify and locate your account:

  • Subject Line: Account Deletion Request
  • Registered Email: The email address associated with your DanceBuddy account.
  • Username: Your DanceBuddy username (if applicable).

Note: For security purposes, please send this request from the same email address that is linked to your DanceBuddy account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When your account is deleted, the following actions take place:

  • Your user profile and login credentials will be permanently removed.
  • All associated data, including your saved dances, preferences, and personal information, will be completely deleted from our active databases and servers.
  • Any videos or content you have uploaded will be permanently deleted from our Cloud Storage.
  • You will no longer receive any emails or notifications from us.

Please note: This process is completely irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Processing Time

In-app deletions take effect immediately. If you request deletion via email, we will process your request and delete your data within 7 days of verifying your identity. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about the account deletion process, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@dancebuddyapp.com.
